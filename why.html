OUR MISSION

WHY WE MADE THIS

A non-profit squash analysis site democratizes elite-level training by providing objective technical feedback that is usually locked behind expensive coaching fees. By using video analysis and data tracking, players can pinpoint flaws in their swing mechanics, court positioning, and movement patterns that are impossible to see in real-time. This level of insight allows athletes at all levels to build smarter tactical habits and track measurable progress, turning what is often a guessing game into a clear, data-driven roadmap for improvement.

Beyond individual growth, the non-profit model fosters a more inclusive global squash community by prioritizing accessibility over revenue. It serves as a central hub for injury prevention and strategic education, particularly for underserved regions or junior programs that lack specialized resources. By focusing on long-term athlete development rather than profit margins, the platform can reinvest in innovative tools and collaborative coaching networks, ultimately raising the standard of play across the entire sport.
