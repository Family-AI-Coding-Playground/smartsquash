THE REASONING

WHY WE BUILT SMARTSQUASH

Squash is one of the most demanding sports in the world — yet it's one of the least supported by modern technology. Football, basketball, and tennis players have access to elite analytics systems worth millions. Squash players get a scoreboard and their memory.

01

The Problem

The average squash player has no way to objectively measure their performance. They rely entirely on feel, gut instinct, and the occasional advice from a hitting partner. Weaknesses go undetected. Good habits go unnoticed. Progress stalls — not from lack of effort, but lack of feedback.

02

The Opportunity

Modern AI can now analyze movement, track fast-moving objects, and generate actionable insights in real time — all from a single smartphone camera. The technology that once required a team of analysts and expensive hardware can now run in your pocket. We saw that and built SmartSquash.

03

The Mission

We believe every squash player — from club beginner to national competitor — deserves access to the same level of insight as elite athletes. SmartSquash exists to democratize performance analysis, close the gap between intention and execution, and help more people fall deeper in love with the sport.

HOW SMARTSQUASH HELPS YOU

Whether you're a beginner stepping onto the court for the first time or a competitive player chasing a ranking, SmartSquash gives you a real edge.

LEARN FASTER THAN EVER

Most players repeat the same mistakes for years because they can't see them. SmartSquash shows you exactly where your technique breaks down — cross-court timing, drop shot depth, court positioning — so every session becomes a lesson, not just exercise. You don't need a coach watching every ball; the app does it for you.

TRAIN WITH A PURPOSE

No more aimless drilling. SmartSquash identifies your weakest patterns — high error zones, overused shots, stamina drop-off points — and turns them into a focused training plan. You'll walk onto the court knowing exactly what to work on and why, making every minute of practice count.

WIN MORE MATCHES

SmartSquash analyzes your opponent's tendencies just as much as yours. Discover that your rival always goes cross-court under pressure, or that your backhand return sets up unforced errors. Enter every match with a game plan built on real data, not guesswork — and finish it knowing exactly what made the difference.

PLAY SMARTER, STAY INJURY-FREE

SmartSquash tracks movement load, swing mechanics, and effort spikes across sessions. It flags when you're overextending on your dominant side or pushing too hard in back-to-back matches — helping you avoid the overuse injuries that sideline players for weeks. Train hard and recover smarter.

YOUR ENTIRE SQUASH CAREER IN ONE PLACE

Every match, every drill, every milestone — stored, searchable, and visualized. Watch yourself improve over months. Share highlight clips with your coach or teammates. SmartSquash is the record your squash career deserves, and the motivation to keep pushing the next level.
